CUTigerPass

Project Conception

CUTigerPass was the brainchild of my experiences working at Clemson University's athletic ticketing office and my love for building applications. Over the four years I spent learning about computers and the day-to-day practices of working in the ticketing division of our athletic program, I learned of the many problems that we faced as an organization. One big one that stood out to me was the fact that students didnt have a place to buy or sell their tickets securely and reliably. So with my skills, I sought to solve this problem

What it Accomplished

CUTigerPass, while still in it's infancy, gives Clemson Students a private venue to privately make sales with one another. Students in the past had to rely on multiple different platforms to make deals which was insecure due to the lack of exclucivity. Once mainstream, CUTigerPass, will be a safe place that people can truly count on.

The platform will also act as an information beacon to students who want to get into the information pool that Clemson Athletics is. They'll be able to get ahead of the game (pun not intended) with the knowledge of when events will be. Under represented athletic programs can get the attention that they need via the knowledge of thier existance. People wont know what they do not know!
